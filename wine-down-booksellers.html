Wine Down Booksellers

Web and Visual Design

Wine Down Booksellers is a web design project for a sample business venture in its early stages that I conceptualized. The main purpose of this site is to promote Wine Down Booksellers, a small independent book and wine merchant in NYC. The site should present the personality of the organization, market their goods, services and events, and encourage visitors to come to their physical location.

Iteration 1 - Persona Creation

User personas were created to during the intial iterations of the design process to represent the business’s target audience. I based the personas off of demographic research focusing on bookstore and wine seller users in the New York City area.


Iteration 2 - Sitemap and User Flow

The site’s main structure is meant to be intuitive for the end-user. Thus, the site content and navigation were designed with simpility in mind to ultimately create a clean, simple user interface.


Iternation 3 - Creating the Look & Feel

The look and feel of the website is sophisticated, friendly, approachable, and playful. The brand’s mood is contemporary and cool. Thus the color scheme was selected to evoke the feelings of sophistication and comfort and the typography and imagry were selected to support the brand.

Iteration 4 - Wireframing

The final iteration of the design process focused on wireframing. I always begin this part of the process with a hand sketched wireframe. The next iterations were created using Balsamiq and Adobe Photoshop.
