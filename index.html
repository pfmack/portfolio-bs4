Swinging with Simone
WordPress Web Design


Swinging with Simone is a WordPress web design project. I worked with my client to create a responsive, user-friendly site that reflected her fun and approachable brand and personality. Wine Down Booksellers
Web & Visual Design


Wine Down Booksellers is a web design project for a sample business venture in its early stages that I conceptualized. The main purpose of this site is to promote Wine Down Booksellers, a small independent book and wine merchant in NYC. The site should present the personality of the organization, market their goods, services and events, and encourage visitors to come to their physical location.
